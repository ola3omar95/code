Prince Mohammed bin Salman, Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia will patronize this evening the ceremony of the most expensive global race "Saudi Cup 2021" in its second edition, at King Abdulaziz Equestrian Square in Al-Janadriyah in Riyadh. The most prominent horses, trainers and cavalry in the world are participating in the championship, whose prize is the most expensive horse race in the world ($30.5 million). The total number of horses participating in King Abdulaziz Square is 77 world horses from 13 countries, with a financial prize of $20 million for first place.
